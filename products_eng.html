LIXX: Wooden table with extra-clear glass top

Designer: Paolo Cicogna

LIXX Table is characterised by a solid wood structure. Although simple as the shape is, the geometrical result is rather complex considering the joint beetween two rhomboidal sections. Changing point of view around the table there are so many effects of intersections that it's easily enjoyable from every perspective, thus being particularly suitable for a central position in the room. The glass top, letting the gaze through, permits to admire the harmonious structure and its sculptural essence.
